Friends:

Family:
I pray for my family and/or adopted family. Your good hand upon us God. Help my immediate family to glorify and please you. I pray for ______________. Give them health, safety, and well being. I pray Your will in their lives. Give them joy in you. Give them strength for dealing with life and their various responsibilities. I pray they have biblical motivations and wisdom. I also pray they have restraint, foresight in their homes and lives. I ask for their salvation and health and safety. For all of us to turn our fears, felt-needs, and frustrations into faith in the Lord Jesus.
